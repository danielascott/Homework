Single meal with an Instant Pot

(it will knock your socks off)

I want to share this recipe for a meal that you can quickly put together and cook in the instant pot. You can adjust the ingredients to how ever many people you would like to cook for

And you are able to swithc out the different cuts of beef or potatoes.

List of ingredients
  • Choice of Steak Cut
  • Water
  • Either Russet or Sweet Potatoes
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Season All
  • Chicken Bouillon Cube
Cooking Directions
  • Add Water to cover steaks
  • Put one Bouillon Cube in water
  • Add onion powder, salt, and season all to your liking
  • Place potatoes on top
  • Put top on instant pot and cook on manual high for 30 minutes
  • Let naturally release for 10 minutes than release pressure manually
